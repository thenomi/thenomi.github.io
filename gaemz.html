What is it?

GÆMZ MEETUP is a place/gathering for people who are interested in the following things:

  • - Developing art/experimental games or playful media of that sort
  • - Thinking about game as an artform
  • - Talking about cool ways to make art for interactive media
  • - Trying to integrate non-game/non-interactive art world into the interactive world
  • - Music games that are not hit-the-bars-you-see
  • - Shaking that button
  • - Alternative villains
  • - Telling me what else to add to this list

What's happening?

Good question. I don't know how it would work yet but I would like to have this meet-up monthly.
I'm vaguely thinking about a structure that includes these things:

  • - Mini 'open mic' session where participants are encouraged to introduce themselves and/or talk about what they are working on/have been working on recently.
  • - Talks (curated)
  • - Something hands-on (e.g. pick one or two games and casually study them together?)

何これ？

GÆMZ MEETUPは下記のようなことが好きな人々用の集まりです:

  • - アート・実験的なゲームまたは他のゲーム的な何かを開発する事
  • - ゲームを芸術として考える事
  • - インタラクティブメディアの為にクールなアートを作る方法
  • - ゲーム外の世界をインタラクティブな世界に取り入れる事
  • - 見えるバーをただ叩くだけじゃない音ゲー
  • - SHAKE THAT BUTTON
  • - 規格外な敵
  • - この他にこのリストに何を加えたらいいか教えてあげる
